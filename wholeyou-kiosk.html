WholeYou™ Terms of Service & Candidate Agreement

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using the WholeYou™ Candidate Evaluation Suite ("the Service"), you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service ("Terms"). If you do not agree, please close your eyes during the evaluation and hope for the best.

2. Data Collection

WholeYou™ collects the following data during your evaluation: facial geometry, estimated age, estimated gender, micro-expressions, blink rate, jawline symmetry, and a general "vibe score" that we cannot fully explain but have found to be statistically significant in ways we'd rather not publish.

3. Use of Biometric Data

Your biometric data will be processed locally and will not be shared with third parties, except our employer partners, their employer partners, data brokers, academic researchers who ask nicely, and a marketing intern who technically shouldn't have access but does.

4. Accuracy Disclaimer

WholeYou™ maintains a 98.6% accuracy rating, which refers to the percentage of the time our system successfully detects that a face is, in fact, a face. Accuracy of personality assessments derived from said face is a separate and ongoing conversation.

5. Consent to Scoring

By proceeding, you acknowledge that your professional worth may be reduced to a single decimal value between 0 and 100, and that this number will be printed on a receipt you can take home and show to your family.

6. Non-Negotiable Terms

The checkbox on the previous page was pre-checked for your convenience. Attempts to uncheck it will be logged, gently corrected, and referenced in your candidate profile under "resistance to standard procedures."

7. Candidate Obligations

During the evaluation window, the candidate agrees to: (a) remain visible to the webcam, (b) refrain from pulling faces, (c) experience exactly one (1) of seven (7) approved emotions, and (d) not think too hard about what is happening.

8. Extended Licensing Agreement

By clicking "Sounds Great, I'm Ready" on any previous screen, you grant WholeYou™ an irrevocable, royalty-free, perpetual license to your facial likeness for use in marketing materials, investor pitch decks, and a motivational poster series titled "Faces of the Future Workforce."

9. Arbitration Clause

All disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration conducted by our proprietary Conflict Resolution Algorithm, which uses the same facial expression analysis model that evaluated you. Smiling during arbitration is strongly recommended.

10. Organ Reservation Program

Candidates who score below 20% on two or more consecutive evaluations automatically enrol in the WholeYou™ Organ Reservation Programme (ORP). By agreeing to these terms, you pre-authorise the reassignment of one (1) non-essential organ from your firstborn child to be determined at a later date.
